Emarsys

Emarsys is a marketing automation platform that helps businesses create and deliver personalized marketing campaigns across multiple channels, including email, mobile, social media, and web. Emarsys uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to analyze customer behavior and provide insights that can be used to improve marketing performance. The platform includes tools for segmentation, email marketing, mobile marketing, social media marketing, web personalization, and analytics. Emarsys was founded in 2000 and is headquartered in Vienna, Austria, with offices around the world. In 2020, Emarsys was acquired by the SAP Customer Experience division of SAP SE. more info about emarsys from here
